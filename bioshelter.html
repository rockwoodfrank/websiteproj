Earthship Bioshelters

If you are interested in using a very renewable home, you may be interested in building an Earthship.

Earthships are build almost entirely off of recycled materials, and have solutions for the six basic human needs: food, energy, clean water, shelter, garbage management, and sewage treatment.

It solves these with creative, sufficient solutions and are completely autonomous. There are also some available for rentals and to visit.

If you would like to learn more, we would love to get in touch with you!

Our Mission

At Sustainable Homes, our mission has always been the same. To help homeowners with building an eco-friendly home, and save the planet. By providing information about solar, wind energy, geothermal, and earthship bioshelters, homeowners are satisfied with their energy choices. Also, our trained professionals that are ready to support these homeowners in any way they can. By doing this, we can help to accomplish our goal and make the Earth a habitable and nice place to live on for years to come.
