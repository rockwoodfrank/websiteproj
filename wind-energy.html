Wind Energy

One of the most popular sources of energy across the world, wind energy is a great alternative to fossil fuels.

Wind energy consists of wind spinning a turbine that is connected to a generator that creates electricity.

Wind energy can be deployed in many ways. Although the most common is a large wind farm that consists of huge wind turbines, usually run by a large company, homeowners can also put up a small turbine for their home.

This may not be a practical option. The Department of Energy recommends that where you live should have an average wind speed of at least ten miles per hour for the turbine to be sufficient.

The wind turbine will also need to be quite tall, at least eighty feet. The upfront cost can be high as well. But if you are in an area that would be sufficient for a wind tower, they are a great source of renewable energy, and it is unlikely you would have to pay an energy bill again.

If you are interested in learning more about renewable energy, we would be glad to help.
