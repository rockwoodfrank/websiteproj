There are plenty of benefits to using renewable energy.

You save money on electricity.

  • The average household spends about $1,856 per year on electricity. All or most of that money can be saved by using solar or wind.
  • On average, a home can save from $400 to $1500 per year using geothermal to warm their home.
  • Although the upfront cost may be high, it's likely you will never have to pay an electricity bill again, and if you do, it won't be what it is now.
  • Any extra electricity you don't use gets sent back to your electric company, and you may be able to receive credit for that electricity.

You're saving the planet.

  • The earth's resources are finite. It is estimated that we will run out of petroleum by 2070, and coal and other fossil fuels by 2110. It is critical that the human race find a new energy source by then.
  • There is no Planet B. There is not another Earth that humans can flee to when we destory this one. We must conserve our resources and choose to use energy that is bot sustainable and infinitely renewable.
  • Renewable resources barely pollute the environment at all compared to fossil fuels.

You get plenty of breaks and benefits.

  • With solar panels, you receive 30% percent back on initial costs as a tax break, meaning a $25,000 setup will save you $7,500.
  • In 2020, you can receive 26% of what you spent on installing geothermal as a tax break.
  • And so much more. It is highly likely that there are even more breaks and bonuses you can get in your area.
