Solar Energy

Solar energy is one of the most common sources of renewable energy, for many reasons.

Solar panels take energy from the sun and turn them into electricity through a technology called photovoltaics. This electricity can be harnessed to power homes, neighborhoods, or even entire cities.

The sun produces a huge amount of energy. Eighteen days of sunshine on the Earth has the same amount of energy as all of the planet’s current reserves of fossil fuels.

It is easy for homeowners to install solar panels on their home. The average setup usually consists of sending the energy you receive to your current electric company, and you are credited for what you send. Any extra energy you don’t use is paid back to you.

Solar panels require very little maintenance after they install, and they cost nothing to run. This means that it is likely you will never have to pay an electricity bill again.
